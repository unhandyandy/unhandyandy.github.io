Three Musketeers





Messages.


This game was invented by Haar Hoolim and first published in Sid Sackson's great collection "A Gamut of Games". The enemy pieces (◎) try to get the 3 musketeers (╋) in a horizontal or vertical line. Musketeers move by capturing a (horizontally or vertically) adjacent piece; the enemy moves by sliding to an empty adjacent space. The musketeers win if they have no possible moves.
